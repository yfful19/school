Our Programs

Our programs are a partnership of parents and teachers working together to support a childs education and well-being.

Donate

Your support helps provide the highest quality of early education and parental support for families who need it most.

PreSchool Partners mission is to provide an inclusive and diverse learning environment that prepares preschool children and their families for success through high-quality curriculum, nurturing educators, parental engagement, and community collaboration. 

Our Mission

Since we were founded in 1995, PreSchool Partners has been a leader in early childhood education, providing school readiness and life skills for under-resourced communities. We believe that every child deserves a quality preschool education, whether their family can afford to pay for it or not. Our program takes a whole-child approach, developing the academic, cognitive, social-emotional, and physical skills children need to be successful in school and beyond.
